Our story

Shelby's Dosa is a small start-up by a group of four friends in Chennai. The love for dosa made us to start this amazing restaurant. This plan was started on 12th of November 2018, which made us to the grand opening on 6th of July 2019 at the Eliot's beach, Beasent nagar, with an amazing name "Shelby's Dosa". Our main idea is to invent as many dishes possible of Dosa.
